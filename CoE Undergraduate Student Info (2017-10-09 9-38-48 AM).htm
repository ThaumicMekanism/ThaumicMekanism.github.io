Faculty Advising is intended primarily for course, graduate school, and industry questions. Faculty members are not expected to answer questions about department policies, petitions, or degree requirements.

Students are not required to attend faculty advising. You must access the web app and opt out. To ensure we can accommodate all students, we ask that you please do this if you intend not to attend a session.

QUESTIONS ABOUT FACULTY ADVISING

KEY DATES AND ACTIONS FOR ALL STUDENTS

October 2-3: For Students who have a rolled over adviser & session assigned below only:
Students will have the opportunity to keep or change their adviser from last semester. If you want to change your adviser for this term, select "(blank)" and click "Save". If you want to keep your adviser, select the faculty’s name and click “Save”. If you opt to select a new adviser, you will be able to select them on October 4th @ 10am. Please note that once the system officially opens on October 4th @ 10am, you will not have the option to opt out or make any other changes. If your previous faculty adviser is not listed, it is likely that your faculty adviser is not available for faculty advising this semester.

October 4 @ 10am: For Students who do not have a rolled over adviser & session assigned below:
Students will be able to select an adviser from the available faculty adviser list. Adviser sign-ups will be on a first-come, first-serve basis.

October 9-20: Faculty Advising occurs

October 16: Phase I begins

Help us collect data about faculty advising. Record the # of people in your session at tinyurl.com/facadvising2017

Note: your choice of an adviser for this term is final and cannot be changed after you click 'Save'.
